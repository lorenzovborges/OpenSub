# Code of Conduct

OpenSub is maintained as a small technical project. Participation should stay
focused, respectful, and useful to the work.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Keep discussions technical and actionable.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about scope, risk, and project direction.
- Avoid sharing private data, account identifiers, credentials, tunnel URLs, or
  exploit payloads in public threads.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Posting secrets, tokens, or exploit instructions in public project spaces.
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith comments after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or
limit participation when behavior creates risk for users or the project.

Security-sensitive reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
